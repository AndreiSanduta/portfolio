About Us

Sanduta Design started with a simple idea to offer people the most pleasant environment possible within their own homes. It all started with the idea that the founder of the project had after renovating his room with just a bucket of paint and some old furniture. The end result was so good that he thought the following: I would like more people to have the same feeling as me, to see the house renovated with little money but with a spectacular end result. With that in mind, he started the project "Sanduta Design" where the main philosophy of the company is to bring the best result with the existing means. Thinking about the environmental issue, especially all furniture and decoration items that are possible to restore and reintegrate into the new decoration is a priority for us.
